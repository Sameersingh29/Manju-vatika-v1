    About

    Welcome to Manju Vatika, where cherished memories blossom and celebrations flourish in a serene embrace. Nestled in nature's lap, our charming wedding venue is a testament to rustic elegance and warm hospitality. With its lush gardens, picturesque surroundings, and thoughtful amenities, Manju Vatika provides the perfect backdrop for your special day. Our caring team is dedicated to ensuring a smooth and joyful experience, carefully tending to every detail of your event. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a festive affair, we adapt our offerings to bring your unique vision to life. Step into our verdant haven and plant the seeds of lifelong memories at Manju Vatika, where nature's beauty enhances the magic of love.
